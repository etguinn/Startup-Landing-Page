Welcome


Recent legislation has required hospitals to release pricing information about their companies.

But there's one problem: only providers are sharing the prices.

How do we know what patients are actually paying?

Enter HealthShare, a secure platform designed to help you get the care you need without hidden or illegible fees.



Our Plan

Stage 1

Collect data from itemized receipts sent in by people like you who want to compare their costs to others.

Stage 2

Invite patients and providers to create accounts and see how their costs compare to others.

Stage 3

Expand the platform to provide patients with recommendations on healthcare they want.

Patients Unite

Patients can access a plethora of healthcare prices at no cost. Just submit your latest receipt from your doctor's visit to create an account and you're ready to go.

Privacy

Your health information is important to us. As such, we make it a priority to keep you safe while using our platform. Through our privacy protection technology, nobody will be able to access your personal information, even if someone steals your account.

Providers

Hospitals can create accounts to interact with users and gather feedback on their services. Participating on our platform gives a powerful way for healthcare providers to connect with their users and learn from them.

Be a part of the revolution.
